About Us!

Our Mission

At Group Search, our mission is to simplify student collaboration by helping users find and join groups that match their goals and interests.

Student Access & Permissions

Students using Group Search can explore available teams and request to join based on shared interests, availability, or class enrollment. They can view group descriptions, current members, and project goals before requesting access.

Team Leader Privileges

Team leaders are those who create new teams or are appointed by their professor. Team Leaders have elevated permissions to manage team dynamics. They can approve or deny join requests, remove members if necessary, and update the project goals.

Professor Oversight & Team Assembly

Professors can oversee all groups associated with their classes. They are able to create new teams, assign students to specific groups and roles, and monitor team membership throughout the course. Professors can add or remove students manually, view group progress, and ensure that team distributions align with course objectives and fairness guidelines.
